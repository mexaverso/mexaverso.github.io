Roadmap

01. Build the DAO and Gather Foundational Community & Funds

Soonaverse has enough functionalities in place to begin Mexaverso DAO, and when the moment arrives we shall decide if Soonaverse is still the specific fit for the full development of the Mexaverso DAO.

Join the community's Discord to see and comment progress in this Roadmap

02.Origin NFT Minting and Upload to Mexaverso's Marketplace

The Origin NFT will have special features. It is the access pass to the Origin Mission version of the Mexaverso App which is an AR (Augmented Reality) app that shows epic AR artwork linked to emblematic places in Mexico. (See proposals).

The Origin NFT will have other special functions, such as giving weight for distribution of earnings pools. And it will possible to rent it!

Enjoy the Origin NFT and Augmented Reality creation progress journey in the YouTube Channel

03. Implementation of NFT renting and sales with Fiat currencies

Any person will be able to buy and rent your NFTs even he or she is not crypto savy.

We will focus also in UX touch-ups so tourists or visitors to the chosen emblematic places can watch the epic Mexaverso AR experiences with a simple smartphone, after easily paying to you.

The completion of this Roadmap milestone means serious earnings for you and is in preparation for the DAO's Main Mission.

04. Full Operation of the DAO to develop and launch projects. Name change?

Tangle technology has a myriad of novel use cases for the decentralized economy. DAO members will harvest profits or get benefits from the created projects.
